Description of functions

Note: All functions except #5, #7 and #8 have hotkeys

  1. Choice of the canvas size
    • You can choose the canvas size, by filling the respective fields of a form "Grid Height" and "Grid Width";
  2. Choice of the cell size
    • You can choose the cell size, by filling the respective field of a form "Cell Size, px";
  3. Choice of the grid visibility
    • You can choose whether to draw a grid on the canvas or not by clicking on the checkbox "Add grid lines?";
  4. Choice of the drawing colors
    • You can choose any colors you want by clicking on color-boxes (1st color — 4th color);
  5. Display of the current colors
    • You can see current colors on the right of the page in "Current colors" section;
  6. Choice of the canvas background
    • You can choose whether to draw a background on the canvas or not by clicking on the button "Add background / Remove background". The current color will become background color of the canvas;
  7. Drawing function
    • You can click on the cell or click and drag your mouse to draw. In other words, every cell under the cursor will be colored while the mouse button is pressed. You can have no worry if the cursor accidentally go outside the canvas while mouse button is pressed. Just go back and continue drawing - your line will not be interrupted;
  8. Erasing function
    • You can erase a color of the cell by combination Shift + left mouse button. The icon of the eraser will appear instead of current color in the "Current colors" section. Click and drag function is also available;
  9. Eyedropper function
    • You can get a color of any colored cell by using Eyedropper. You can find this button on the control panel. At first click on the Eyedropper button, then click on a cell with necessary color you want to get. If you try to click with active eyedropper on the cell that have no color, you will receive an error message. (Don't worry. It's just a pop-up notification window);
  10. Undo-Redo function
    • You can undo and redo few changes that have been made. The number of undo-redo operations is limited depends on the canvas size. You can find these buttons on the control panel;
  11. Clear and delete canvas function
    • You can use this function by clicking on "Clear / Delete canvas" button. If your canvas has at least one colored cell you can clear all the canvas by clicking on "Clear canvas" button. If your canvas has no colored cells by clicking on the "Delete canvas" button. Your confirmation is required after every click on this button that you are really ready to clear the canvas. WARNING! This operation cannot be undone! If you try to click on this button when there is no canvas on the page you will receive an error message. (Don't worry. It's just a pop-up notification window).

List of hotkeys

  1. Move focus to "Grid Height" form field: H
  2. Move focus to "Grid Width" form field: W
  3. Move focus to "Cell Size" form field: S
  4. Toggle grid visibility: G
  5. Active color keys according to numbers of colors: 1 — 4 (e.g. : 1 == 1st color)
  6. Toggle canvas background: B
  7. Toggle eyedropper mode: I
  8. Undo changes: Ctrl + Z
  9. Redo changes: Ctrl + Y
  10. Clear / delete canvas: Del
  11. Close pop-up modal window with no changes: Esc
